Petroleum Equipment Services is a multifaceted company that supplies both foreign and domestic markets with top quality products and services in the oil, gas, and geothermal industries. Our vast network of vendors, contractors, and various suppliers is a powerful resource that can aid you in completing your projects under budget and on time, every time. Like any procurement company, Petroleum Equipment Services can supply drilling equipment and productions but our true value is in service. Petroleum Equipment Services is a experienced and efficient company, able to move quickly to meet the goals of its clients. Petroleum Equipment Services' procurement specialists have over a 100 years combined experience in the petroleum industry. With the experience of people who do what they love, and the connections that matter, Petroleum Equipment Services has helped supply material and equipment to job locations all over the world.
