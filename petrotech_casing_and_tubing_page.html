Casing and Tubing















Casing and tubing are fundamental components in the oil and gas industry, playing crucial roles in well construction and production. These steel pipes are used to line the borehole and transport hydrocarbons to the surface, ensuring the integrity and efficiency of the well.

Casing:
  • Conductor Casing
  • Surface Casing
  • Intermediate Casing
  • Production Casing


Tubing:
  • Standard Tubing
  • Coiled Tubing
  • Corrosion-Reistant Tubing
